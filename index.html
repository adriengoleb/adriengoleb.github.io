Adrien Golebiewski is an AI Research Scientist at Lerity and Ph.D. Candidate in the Centre for Mathematical Morphology (CMM) at the Ecole des Mines Paris - PSL. His research interests lie in the area of Scene understanding, at the intersection of Computer Vision, and Deep learning.
He has held research and engineer positions with several industrial organisations, including Lerity, Safran and Dassault Aviation.
